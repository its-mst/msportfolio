Recipe for Chapati Beef Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 kg beef
  • 500g Exe Wheat Flour
  • salt
  • Frying oil
  • Grated carrots
  • 3 Ripe Tomatoes
  • 1 tbs curry powder
  • 2 onions
  • Coriander Bunch

Cooking Instructions

Chapati

  1. Boil three cps of water and add salt
  3. Put the wheat flour in a bowl, add grated carrota and mix well
  4. Add 5 spoons of cooking oil and mix
  5. Add the boiled water slowly as you knead to form a soft dough
  6. Lay the dough and cut circular balls then respectively roll into a circular shape
  7. Heat the frying pan and start frying the circled dough till the bottom turns brownish while adding one spoon of frying oil
  8. Turn to the other side and carry out the same procedure

Beef Curry

  1. Boil the meat for 15 minutes to soften
  2. When the meat is ready, chop your onions, tomatoes and coriander
  3. Add two spoons of frying oil into a sufuria and heat for 3 second
  4. Add the chopped onions,tomatoes and coriander and fry till soft
  5. Add the meat and curry powder and fry for two minutes
  6. Add a little water for soup
  7. Serve with Chapati and Enjoy!
